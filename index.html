About Abdulsalam Shehu Jaji

Abdulsalam Shehu Jaji was born in Ile Jaji, Ogba, Idi Ape, in Magagi Aare Ward. His journey began with a solid educational foundation that has shaped his professional trajectory and expertise in business administration and economic development.

With a diverse educational background spanning accounting, economics, and business administration, Jaji has cultivated a multifaceted skill set that has enabled him to excel in various leadership roles throughout his career.

Currently serving as the Chief Executive Officer of Pagers World Global Construction and Crome Nigeria Enterprise, Jaji continues to leverage his expertise to drive innovation and growth in the business sector while exploring opportunities to contribute to public policy and governance.
